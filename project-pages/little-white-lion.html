This is an interactive design for a novelty app called Little White Lion.

The purpose of Little White Lion is to generate excuses for you that have been submitted by other users. You can add to your favorites or upvote and downvote those which may or may not have worked for you. There are different categories for different types of situations.Who are you trying to avoid?
