MyMenu

Photography, Illustrator, Photoshop

This is an interactive design for an app that helps you take whatever inventory you may have in your kitchen and gives you recipe options for breakfast, lunch or dinner.

Previous Project
Next Project

This app is able to do three things: Create a grocery list, once purchased add groceries to your inventory directly from said list, and then the app takes what you have and give you options for your breakfast, lunch or dinner. As you scroll through your options you can see that it shows you some of the first things you want to know when cooking dinner: A brief description, Preparation Time, Calories, Rating, and Difficulty. Whether you’re trying new ideas or just don't know what to have, this app will help you out.
