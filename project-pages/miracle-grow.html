Miracle Grow

Photography, Photoshop, Illustrator

This is an Ad Campaign containing 3 print components, an online component, and an environmental component for a plant food called Miracle Grow.

Previous Project
Next Project

Miracle Grow's ad concepts are currently about bringing families together, but to me that doesn't explain to the new user what Miracle Grow is or does, which is help plants, trees and vegetables grow larger and fuller. Right now it's reaching toward older generations that have grandchildren. I wanted to reach out to the younger mothers who would be getting into gardening. I wanted to introduce to this new audience exactly what Miracle Grow is and what it can do for them. This new ad campaign shows just that.
