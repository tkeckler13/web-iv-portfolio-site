Redneck Triatholon

Illustration, InDesign, Photoshop

This is a promotional design for an annual local event in my hometown called the Redneck Triatholon.

Previous Project
Next Project

The Redneck Triatholon is a race consisting of multiple obstacles that we do every year during Limestone Days! This year it's being sponsored by Keckler Oil Co. and I wanted to put some of Keckler's style into the design. The people usually have exaggerated features with unproportionate bodies. I used inked out the character on paper and did the rest in InDesign. The t–shirts are what the first ten participants to cross the line would receive.
